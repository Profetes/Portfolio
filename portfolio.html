FBLA: Revival

FBLA: Revival is a video game entry for the 2024 State and National FBLA Computer Game & Simulation Programming Competition. FBLA: Revival is made in Unreal Engine using Blueprints, a visual scripting, C++ based programming language. FBLA:Revival is the most complex project I have undertaken, as all assets are custom. Everything from models, to code, to effects, and to music are made by myself. See a video overview (voiced by a friend)
